After starting GrowthBook with Docker, the first thing to do is create the admin account and set up your organization.

1. Open `http://localhost:3000` (or your Cloud URL) in your browser.
2. Click **Get Started** or **Register** — you will be prompted to create the first admin account. Enter your name, email, and password.
3. Create your **Organization** — give it a name (e.g., "My Company"). This is the top-level container for everything in GrowthBook.
4. (Optional) Create a **Project** — Projects let you group features and experiments within an organization. Click **Create Project** and give it a name.
5. Review the default **Environments**: GrowthBook creates `development`, `staging`, and `production` by default. You can add or rename them under **Settings → Environments**.
6. Explore the left sidebar:
   - **Features** — your feature flags.
   - **Experiments** — A/B tests.
   - **Metrics** — define what success looks like.
   - **Settings** — SDK connections, environments, team members, data sources.

<Callout title="Key point">
The first account you create becomes the organization admin. There is no separate admin-creation step — whoever registers first owns the organization.
</Callout>
